Seam Carver

A seam carving application for content-aware image resizing.



Seam carving is a content-aware image resizing technique that intelligently identifies and removes the least important pixels from an image, allowing for non-uniform scaling while preserving important content.


Key Features:

Content-Aware Resizing:Seam carving adapts to the content of an image, preserving important features and details during the resizing process.
Real-time Visualization: The project includes animated GIFs that vividly illustrate how seams are intelligently carved from the image, with red lines highlighting the pixels being removed.
Vertical and Horizontal Carving: Seam carving is performed in both vertical and horizontal directions, providing flexibility in adjusting the image size based on specific requirements.
Energy Visualization:Explore the energy visualization of the image, showcasing the algorithm's understanding of the importance of each pixel in the context of its surroundings.
Efficiency and Optimization: The project includes optimizations to enhance the efficiency of the seam carving process, ensuring a smoother experience even for high-resolution images.
Technologies Used: Programming Language: Python
Libraries: NumPy, PIL (Pillow), tqdm
